Time and habits

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

The timeboxing really saved me with that rad typing game. The pomodoro technique (which is currently ticking away) for time management.

For habit techniques, I've embraced the four questions, and I intend to start a tiny habit using the Fogg method as soon as I come up with one that isn't just flossing.

What did you find interesting?

I found the habit forming content the most interesting. Not least because I feel it's something I could really benefit from. In my past the only habit I've formed with habit forming techniques is consistently failing to keep up the habit.

What worked for you?

I find the pomodoro technique most helpful. It can however be a little distracting when the timer goes off mid-thought. Or worse, when I see there's only a minute left so I get the old 'better hurry up and finish this thing before the time runs out oh look I'm paying more attention to the timer than the material' situation.

Which will you experiment further with?

I intend to give the four questions are real go. Let's see how it works out. In the last year I've also dabbled in mindfulness meditation. I hope to pick up that again more routinely.
